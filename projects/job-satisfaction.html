Accessing Most Signficant Job Satisfaction Facets in Software Developers Turnover

Abstract: Since the turnover of IT professionals percentile is very high according to LinkedIn (2018), technology (software) had the highest turnover rate with 13.2% this is a topic extensively studied over the years. The goal of the experiment was to access which facets of job satisfaction were the most significant for the software developers in the decision of turnover. We collected data from the Love Mondays (now, GlassDoor) website using a web scraper, analysed the data using ABSA (Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis) with Keras. The results shows that facets of job satisfaction that made software developers tick are related mostly on operational conditions, communication and promotions.
